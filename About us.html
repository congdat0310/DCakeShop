About Us

DCake Company was established in May 2020 and in December 2020 we officially become a cake distribution agent.

For the main purpose of offering customers different cakes to meet the needs of eating and learning about Vietnamese cakes, DCake Company has established a large organization with great human resources. One year after the establishment of the company, we began to create website for DCake and expand the scope of business nationwide.

In the future, DCake intends to expand branches internationally, provide more cakes, develop a beter quality website and best meet the services and requirements of customers.
